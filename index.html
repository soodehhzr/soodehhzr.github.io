Greetings! I'm Soodeh, and welcome to my website!

I was born in a musical family. My father was a multi-instrumentalist. He was an advanced pianist and a skillful percussionist. I still have books containing the melodies that he had written. Sadly, my father passed away when I was 3 years old. However, I feel that I have inherited “the muse” from him, since I have been constantly engaged in musical activities since my childhood. In my early years, I took piano classes and mastered my playing skills.

When I was studying at highschool, I found out that I am so enthusiastic about teaching topics that I was good at. That became more clear when I decided to work with children. That's why I acquired the TTC certificate, so I can teach kids the basics of the english language. Besides that, I have been introducing many children to music by teaching beginner piano lessons. I just absolutely love it while seeing little children take their first steps in music.

In my last year of high school, since there are not many decent music schools in Iran, I consulted with my teachers and advisors for considering alternatives, and they recommended me to pursue a management major in the university. I took their advice and enrolled in a Industrial Management bachelor’s program at Ershad University. This program, in addition to the work experience that I acquired afterwards gave me so many insights, amongst them, how to efficiently manage employees, organize a group of people. But yet there was something missing, Music.

That was the reason for enrolling in a two-years program for Classical Music Performance at the University of Applied Science. I managed to score more than 80 percent marks, hence I am one of the top ranks among all students. While studying, I also decided to increase the amount of piano students that I had. Receiving positive feedback pushed me even further, to the point where I decided to steer my career towards music even more. Thus, during COVID quarantine period, I also enrolled in an online Pedagogy program at University of Zurich, and managed to finish it with merit and obtain the certification.

Apart from all that, I played at multiple piano recital performances, composed a few songs, and accompanied other artists from all over the world in creating music and jamming performances.
